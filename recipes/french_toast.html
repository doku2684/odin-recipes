French Toast

Description

"This fabulous French toast recipe works with many types of bread â€” white, whole wheat, brioche, cinnamon-raisin, Italian, or French! Delicious served hot with butter and maple syrup."

Ingredients

  • 2/3 Cup of Milk
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 6 Think Slices of Bread
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 Tablesppon of Unsalted Butter
  • 1 Teaspoon of Vanilla Extract (Optional)
  • 1/4 Teaspoon of Ground Cinnamon (Optional)

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Whisk milk, eggs, vanilla, cinnamon, and salt together in a shallow bowl.
  3. Lightly butter a griddle or skillet and heat over medium-high heat.
  4. Dunk bread in the egg mixture, soaking both sides.
  5. Transfer to the hot skillet and cook until golden, 3 to 4 minutes per side.
  6. Serve hot.
